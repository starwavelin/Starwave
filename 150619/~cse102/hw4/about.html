About NY-Fujian Tourism LLC

NY-Fujian Tourism LLC is a tourism company founded in 2009. Its objective is to provide people in New York State with the traveling plans to Fujian province, China.

Key Figures


Mr. Xian Lin


President


Mr. Xian Lin, the co-founder of NY-Fujian Tourism LLC, received his bachelor degree in Information Systems from Stony Brook University in the year 2009. When he was in college, he had special insight in running touring business. He used to serve as a campus tour guide for new-admitted international students each summer at Stony Brook University.


Mr. Richard Sun


Vice President


Mr. Richard, the co-founder of NY-Fujian Tourism LLC, graduated from Peking University in 2007. He further studied at Stony Brook University for his master degree in financial engineering. In the year 2009, he started this business with Mr. Xian Lin.


Ms. Sydney Zhao


Financial Officer


Ms. Zhao graduated from Xiamen University in 2009, holding a bachelor degree in accounting. She joined NY-Fujian Tourism LLC in 2010.
