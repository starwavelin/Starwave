Mountain Wuyi

Good Spots for Visiting


Nine Turnings River

Unlike Fuzhou City and Xiamen City, if you pay a visit to Wuyi Mountain, you would enjoy many great natural landscape and humanity places of interest at the same time.

Although the name of the river wrapping Wuyi Mountain is known as Nine Turnings River, that does not necessary mean that the river has nine turns. In Chinese convention, number nine represents multiplicity. You can take a bamboo raft to view the landscape on the river.


Ancient Porcelain Factory

The image on the right shows an ancient porcelain factory dated to the Northern Song Dynasty (960-1127). You can still see some unfinished porcelain containers on the land between steps. Probably these unfinished products are the ancestors of later well known Blue and White Porcelain.


Da Hong Pao Tea

Ladies who are picking up tea leaves

Another place or actually item you will not want to miss in Wuyi Mountain is the Da Hong Pao Tea. Da Hong Pao Tea is the foremost oolong teas whose tea trees only exist in Wuyi Mountain. In the past, only the Chinese emperor could have this type of teas to drink, due to the very limited amount of production. Now, you can see how valuable it is!
