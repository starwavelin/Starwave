Company News

April 01, 2011


From left to right:
Xian Lin, Richard Sun, Sydney Zhao, Tiffany Cai,
Thomas Robinson, Timothy Swifter, Benjamin Ng

After the three day negotiation between representatives from U.S.-Aisa Global Travel Corporation and NY-Fujian Tourism LLC, the CEO of NY-Fujian Tourism LLC, Mr. Xian Lin, agreed with the plan that U.S.-Asia Global Travel Corporation will acquire NY-Fujian Tourism LLC in July 2011 as its sub-company. All current employees of NY-Fujian Tourism LLC would retain their positions and receive $10,000 bonus to their annual income.

NEW Travel Lines

Starting on May 23, 2011, the following three travel lines would be available:

  1. New York City -> Taipei -> Fuzhou
  2. Stony Brook -> Fuzhou -> Xiamen
  3. Princeton -> Fuzhou -> Quanzhou -> Wuyi Mountain

HOT Travel Lines

Based on the 2010 annual statistics, the most favorable travel lines provided by NY-Fujian Tourism LLC are as follows:

  • New York City -> Fuzhou -> Xiamen
  • Stony Brook -> Fuzhou -> Wuyi Mountain
